ABOUT US

MAKVEN UNITED is a ONE STOP SOLUTION PLATFORM in India for any health, fitness and gym equipment needs for residential, commercial, corporate & institutional clients. Our expertise is in providing tailor made and fully customized solutions for any fitness related equipment requirements across all segments of our society.
While we provide clients with solutions, we would also be offering the stakeholders the right motivation to strive for excellence and make the investment profitable to investors.


OUR VISION

Our vision is to spread the importance of health & fitness by promoting entertaining, educational, friendly and innovative solutions of uncompromising quality to meet the needs for the well being of our society.
MAKVEN wishes to become an agent of change in our society to lead and support people in their pursuit of a healthy mind, body, and soul.. We envisage to become one of main pillars of “Make in India, Make for World” drive expected to develop India to become self-reliant and use its vast natural resources to produce for the domestic as well as the global market.
